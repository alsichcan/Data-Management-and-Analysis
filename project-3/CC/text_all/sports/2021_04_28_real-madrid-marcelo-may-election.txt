Real Madrid’s Marcelo Freed From Election Duty and Can Face Chelsea
UPDATE, May 4: Marcelo was replaced at the polling station on Tuesday and traveled with his teammates to London for Wednesday’s match.Real Madrid left back Marcelo was released from his duty at a Spanish polling station on Tuesday, freeing him to travel with his team to London for a Champions League semifinal match against Chelsea on Wednesday.Marcelo had been randomly selected to work a shift at the polls during local elections in Madrid and arrived as scheduled, but he was allowed to leave after only a few minutes when another citizen volunteered to take his place.
