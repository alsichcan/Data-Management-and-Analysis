Blue Jays Capture Series Thanks to Homers by Guerrero, Grichuk
Vladimir Guerrero Jr. dropped 42 pounds after an underwhelming 2020.He’s hoping less can be more — and the 22-year-old still looks like a heavy hitter.Guerrero and Randal Grichuk homered off Domingo German in the Yankee right-hander’s return from a domestic violence ban, and the Toronto Blue Jays beat the Yankees, 3-1, Sunday to win a season-opening series against their A.L. East rival.A slimmed-down Guerrero had a hit in all three games, including an opposite-field shot leading off the second inning against German.
