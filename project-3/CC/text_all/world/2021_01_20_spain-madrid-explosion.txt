Madrid Explosion Leaves at Least 3 Dead
An explosion tore through a building in central Madrid on Wednesday, leaving at least three people dead and 11 more wounded, Spanish officials said, as rescuers scrambled to treat the injured.The mayor of Madrid, José Luis Martínez-Almeida, told local news outlets that the explosion appeared to be the result of a gas leak, and the Interior Ministry said later that it was investigating the cause. Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez of Spain acknowledged the explosion in a post on Twitter, and shared a warning from the police urging residents to stay away from the scene.
