8 Immigrants Killed in Border City Crash
 Eight immigrants were killed on Monday afternoon when a pickup truck they were riding in crashed head-on into another pickup during a police chase in southwestern Texas, the authorities said on Tuesday amid a deepening humanitarian crisis along the Mexican border.The accident occurred on U.S. Highway 277 about 30 miles north of the border city Del Rio, according to the Texas Department of Public Safety, which said that the Dodge pickup with the immigrants had evaded Texas State Troopers before the crash.The authorities did not elaborate on what had led to the chase, but said the Dodge had caused a head-on collision with a Ford F-150.The eight passengers in the Dodge who were killed, all undocumented immigrants, died at the scene, according to the Department of Public Safety.
