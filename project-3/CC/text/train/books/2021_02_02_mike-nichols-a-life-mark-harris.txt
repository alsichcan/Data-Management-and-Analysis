Mike Nichols’s Brilliant Career
MIKE NICHOLSA LifeBy Mark HarrisIndividual genius is a group accomplishment, and don’t let Flaubert tell you different. Mike Nichols, whose name stands alone on the marquee of Mark Harris’s gleaming, teeming biography, possessed wit, cunning, icy clarity, ravenous ambition and a knack for mimicking the qualms and quavers of neurosis, but none of that would have been set free if he hadn’t lucked upon the right tribe.
