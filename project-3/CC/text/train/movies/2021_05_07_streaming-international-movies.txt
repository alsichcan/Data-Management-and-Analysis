Five International Movies to Stream Right Now
In the age of streaming, the Earth is flat — screen-size, with travel to faraway destinations only a monthly subscription and a click away. But sifting the wheat from the chaff can be hard with so many options, and harder still if you don’t know what to look for in the bounties of different national cinemas and film industries.So let me be your travel agent: I’ve journeyed through the world of streaming and chosen the best new international movies for you to watch. This month’s picks include an Indian Künstlerroman, a Japanese antiwar wild ride, a campy Nigerian take on Kafka’s “The Metamorphosis,” and more.
