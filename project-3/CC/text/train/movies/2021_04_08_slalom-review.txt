‘Slalom’ Review: First, Abuse, Then a Steep Downhill
In competitive skiing, athletes balance the rewards of downhill glory against the dangers of a fall. The sensitive, discomforting drama “Slalom” follows Lyz (Noée Abita), a 15-year-old recruit to a ski facility in the French Alps. There, young skiers are molded into champions by an ambitious trainer, Fred (Jérémie Renier).From their first meeting, the relationship between Lyz and Fred is physical. Fred asks Lyz to undress so he can monitor her weight, her musculature, her menstrual cycle, her fitness. Lyz blossoms under his attention. Her skiing improves, and she begins to win tournaments.
