Kamala Harris says the Atlanta attacks have ‘frightened and shocked and outraged all people.’
Authorities, Atlanta residents and Asian-American groups across the country reacted with shock and outrage to the massage parlor shootings that killed eight people on Tuesday evening.Vice President Kamala Harris, the first woman and the first Asian-American to hold the office, expressed condolences for the families of the victims during a meeting with Irish officials on Wednesday. “This speaks to a larger issue which is the issue of violence in our country and what we must do to never tolerate it and to always speak out against it,” Ms. Harris said.“I do want to say to our Asian-American community that we stand with you and understand how this has frightened and shocked and outraged all people,” she added.
